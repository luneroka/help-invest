Understanding your level of risk tolerance is an essential part of developing your investment strategy. Two important aspects to evaluate when determining your risk profile are your investment time horizon and how you emotionally react to changes in the market.

What is a risk profile?

Your risk profile reflects both your willingness and capacity to take on risk in your investments, making it a key factor to consider when shaping your investment strategy. Understanding your risk profile allows you to make investment choices that align with your comfort level and support your progress toward achieving your financial goals.
